Admission

Undergraduate Admission




Degree Offered: Bachelor of Science (BSc), 4-year Program.


The admission process for prospective undergraduate students is not directly handled by the department authority. Therefore, we strongly recommend that you go through the Official BRUR Website for information regarding admission procedures and related announcements. However, you will find a brief guideline about the process on this page.
Currently, there are six units through which a student can select the subject after appearing in the admission exams. These exams are quite competitive as the number of available seats are limited. The department of Computer Science and Engineering in Begum Rokeya University, Rangpur, also known as CSE BRUR, is under Faculty of Engineering and Technology and the admission seeking students must appear in the E unit exam for admission. There are 45 seats in CSE BRUR. The process of admission starts after the result of Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) or equivalent (in case of O/A level and International students) Examination of all education board. As part of that process, the admission committee publishes circular for the admission on national newspapers and University's official website.


Admission Timeline (Tentative)
Application Begins/Ends Admission Test Admission Test Result
September-November December Usually within few days after the test



There are some rules and regulations must be followed by the applicants. The students must be from science group obtaining at least GPA 3.5 (as of 2016) in both Secondary School Certificate (SSC) and Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) or equivalent Examination.

After the admission examination, a merit list will be published based on the score of the exam and the summation of credits of SSC and HSC exams. Note that, a student can be admitted in the department of Computer Science and Engineering based on the students merit position and a minimum score of 15 in both the Physics and Mathematic and minimum 7 in English on the test. However, the admission requirements are changed over years and are published on the University website. Please visit Official Page of Admission Test @BRUR for details about the admission of the upcoming session.


International Students/ O/A Level Students/ Open U Students*

There are specific instructions for International / O/A Level and Open U Students each year which is usually provided in the admission circular of that particular year's admission examination prospectus. Interested applicants of a particular year's admission from these categories are strongly advised to consult the website of BRUR and ask their queries on the Official Page of Admission Test @BRUR
